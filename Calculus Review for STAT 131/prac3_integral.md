Practice Problems: Integrals
================

1.  Evaluate the definite integral: $$\int_0^2 (3x^2 + 4x - 2) \, dx$$

2.  Evaluate: $$\int_0^1 x(1 + x)^3 \, dx$$

    - *(Hint: Either expand the polynomial first, or use substitution
      with $u = 1+x$).*

3.  Consider the function: $$
    f(x) = \begin{cases} 
    2(3x - x^2), & 0 < x < 3, \\
    0, & \text{otherwise}.
    \end{cases}
    $$ Evaluate $\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x) \, dx$.

    - *(Hint: Pay attention to the support).*

4.  Define: $$
    f(x) = \begin{cases} 
    1/5, & 1 \le x \le 6, \\
    0, & \text{otherwise}.
    \end{cases}
    $$

    1)  Evaluate $\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} f(x) \, dx$.
    2)  Compute $\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} x f(x) \, dx$.

    - *(Hint: Pay attention to the support).*

5.  Define: $$
    f(x) = \begin{cases} 
    e^{-3x}, & x \ge 0, \\
    0, & x < 0.
    \end{cases}
    $$ Evaluate $\int_{-\infty}^{a} f(x) \, dx$ for any given $a > 0$.

6.  Let: $$
    f(x) = \begin{cases} 
    x e^{-x^2}, & x \ge 0, \\
    0, & x < 0.
    \end{cases}
    $$ Evaluate $\int_{-\infty}^{a} f(x) \, dx$ for any given $a > 0$.

7.  Evaluate the following integral: $$\int_0^2 x e^{-x} \, dx$$

    - *(Hint: Use Integration by Parts with $u = x$ and
      $dv = e^{-x} dx$).*

8.  Evaluate the following integral:
    $$\int_0^{\infty} x^3 e^{-x^2} \, dx$$

    - *(Hint: First substitute $u = x^2$, then use Integration by
      Parts).*

9.  Evaluate: $$\int_1^e \frac{\log(x)}{x} \, dx$$

    - *(Hint: Use substitution with $u = \log(x)$).*

10. Evaluate: $$\int_0^{\pi} x \sin(x) \, dx$$

11. Evaluate: $$\int_1^{\infty} \frac{1}{x^3} \, dx$$

12. Evaluate: $$\int_0^1 x^2 (1 - x)^2 \, dx$$

13. Evaluate: $$\int_0^{\infty} x e^{-2x} \, dx$$

14. Find the value of the constant $C$ such that:
    $$\int_0^1 C x^2 \, dx = 1$$

    - *(Hint: Evaluate the integral in terms of $C$, set it equal to 1,
      and solve for $C$).*
